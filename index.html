Plugins
  • PlayerPoints: Use `/points` to manage and trade with points.
  • GSit: Adds emotes like `/sit`, `/lay`, and `/crawl`.
  • SimpleTpa: Teleport with `/tpa`, `/tpaccept`, `/tpdeny`, `/tpahere`.
  • BetterTimber: Break a tree base to chop it all down.
  • Grave Plugin: Items saved in a grave when you die.
  • GriefPrevention: Claim land with a golden shovel and protect your builds.
  • EconomyShopGUI: Use `/shop`, `/sellall`, `/balance`, and `/pay`.
  • HuskHomes: Set homes and warp with `/home`, `/sethome`, `/warp`, `/spawn`.
  • PlayerKits2: Access starter kits with `/kits` and `/kit `.
  • Simple Voice Chat: Press V to use in-game voice chat (requires mod).
