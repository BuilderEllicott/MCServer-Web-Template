Plugins

  • PlayerPoints: `/points`, `/points look`, `/points pay <player> <amount>` – Track and trade points as currency.
  • GSit: `/sit`, `/lay`, `/crawl` – Roleplay emotes for fun and immersion.
  • SimpleTpa: `/tpa`, `/tpaccept`, `/tpdeny`, `/tpahere` – Easy teleportation system.
  • BetterTimber: Chop the base of trees to fell them quickly.
  • Grave Plugin: Keeps your items in a grave when you die.
  • GriefPrevention: Use a golden shovel to claim land and protect builds.
  • EconomyShopGUI: `/shop`, `/sellall`, `/balance`, `/pay` – GUI shop economy system.
  • HuskHomes: `/home`, `/sethome`, `/warp`, `/spawn`, `/tpa` – Manage homes and warps.
  • PlayerKits2: `/kits`, `/kit <name>` – Preconfigured item kits.
  • Simple Voice Chat: Press **V** in-game to use mic (mod required).
